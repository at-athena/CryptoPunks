CryptoPunks Tokenomics

CryptoPunks: Where Pixels Meet Ownership, Defining Rarity in the Digital Realm.

Fixed Supply

There are exactly 10,000 CryptoPunks and no more can ever be created, ensuring scarcity.

Unique Attributes

Each CryptoPunk has distinct characteristics that can make some more rare and valuable than others.

Ownership

When a CryptoPunk is purchased, the ownership is transferred and recorded on the Ethereum blockchain.

Initial Distribution

Originally, CryptoPunks were claimed for free by anyone with an Ethereum wallet when they were released in 2017.

Secondary Market

CryptoPunks can be bought and sold on secondary markets, with prices determined by demand.

Market Dynamics

The value of individual CryptoPunks is influenced by rarity, market sentiment, and the overall NFT market trends.

No Divisibility

Unlike cryptocurrencies that can be divided into smaller units, each CryptoPunk is indivisible and can only be owned in whole.

No Staking or Rewards

There are no mechanisms like staking or passive income directly associated with owning a CryptoPunk.

Community Value

The value of CryptoPunks is also driven by the community and cultural significance within the crypto and art world.

About Us

We AreCryptoPunks

CryptoPunks is a pioneering non-fungible token (NFT) project on the Ethereum blockchain, featuring 10,000 unique, algorithmically generated 24x24 pixel art characters. These digital collectibles have various distinct attributes and are considered one of the earliest examples of NFTs, which have ownership rights secured by the blockchain. CryptoPunks has a fixed supply of characters, making them a sought-after digital asset for collectors and investors. The project has gained a cult following and is seen as a foundational piece of the crypto art movement, with some punks selling for millions of dollars at auction. It represents a blend of art, technology, and finance, capturing the ethos of digital ownership and the value of rarity in the digital age.
